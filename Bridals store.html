      LUV BRIDAL EXPERIENCE

      From the moment you walk into LUV Bridal, we aim to give you and your loved ones the experience of a lifetime. With one-on-one friendly and passionate service, chic and fresh Showrooms, and multiple sizes of inclusive samples for a near-perfect fit, you will be sure to have a stress-free, exciting, and bride-focused day.

      Here you will feel welcomed and understood before you've even started looking for your dress.

      SHOWROOMS

      For a unique and memorable bridal shopping experience, look no further than our luxurious Showrooms. Located in California, Arizona, Florida, and Colorado, each of our stores flaunts its own personality, but features spacious styling suites and plenty of seating for your loved ones.

      With highly trained stylists, known for their knowledge and charm, and endless rows of designer wedding dresses that suit an array of different styles and body types, you'll be sure to find the dress of your dreams..
